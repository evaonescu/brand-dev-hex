ABOUT US



       Our Vision

 

In a world where people are limiting their uniqueness in order to fit in, we believe that only when we are unafraid to show who we really are, we can break the chains of social standards together and bring to light the beauty of individuals and their differences.

Our Concept

How does it work? Our concept is simple: white sneakers that can have a personalized message and the art of a customer on them. Moreover, when clients submit their art they can allow other people to buy shoes with it as well, but each pair is unique, so there will only be 1 of each size available with every model! Artists can gain visibility and simple people are empowered to express themselves. In addition, on the inside of the shoe there is a hex code that will provide access to a special part of the discord server - a tight community of like-minded people. Through our social campaigns, we will encourage people to stop being afraid of their uniqueness, to express themselves through art, and to connect with our community!
